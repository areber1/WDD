About Our Auto Shop

AAMCO Transmissions and Total Car Care is a well-known automotive service franchise that specializes in transmission repairs and maintenance, as well as providing comprehensive automotive care services. Here's a description of what AAMCO Transmissions and Total Car Care does:

Transmission Services: AAMCO is particularly renowned for its expertise in transmission repair, rebuilding, and replacement. They have highly trained technicians who can diagnose and address various transmission issues, such as slipping gears, leaking fluids, and unusual noises. AAMCO offers both manual and automatic transmission services, including repairs, maintenance, and complete rebuilds.

Total Car Care: In addition to their transmission specialization, AAMCO also offers a wide range of automotive services to address other vehicle maintenance and repair needs. These services include engine diagnostics and repairs, brake system repairs, suspension and steering repairs, heating and cooling system maintenance, electrical system diagnostics, and general automotive maintenance such as oil changes, tire rotations, and fluid flushes.

Free Initial Vehicle Check: AAMCO provides a free initial vehicle check to customers who visit their centers. This comprehensive evaluation helps identify any immediate or potential issues with the transmission and other key systems of the vehicle. It allows customers to have a clear understanding of the overall condition of their vehicle and make informed decisions regarding necessary repairs or maintenance.

Nationwide Warranty: AAMCO offers a nationwide warranty for its transmission services, providing customers with peace of mind. The warranty covers a specified period and mileage, ensuring that customers are protected against any transmission-related issues that may arise after the service.

Professional Technicians and State-of-the-Art Equipment: AAMCO ensures that its technicians are well-trained and equipped with the knowledge and expertise to handle a wide range of automotive repairs and maintenance tasks. They utilize advanced diagnostic tools and equipment to accurately diagnose and repair vehicles, ensuring efficient and effective service.

Customer Service and Education: AAMCO emphasizes providing exceptional customer service and building trust with their customers. They strive to educate customers about their vehicle's needs, explaining the issues and repair options in a clear and understandable manner. AAMCO aims to empower customers to make informed decisions about their vehicle maintenance and repair.

Overall, AAMCO Transmissions and Total Car Care is a trusted provider of transmission repairs and maintenance, as well as a comprehensive range of automotive services. They combine their expertise in transmissions with a dedication to customer service and quality workmanship to ensure that customers receive reliable and efficient automotive care.
